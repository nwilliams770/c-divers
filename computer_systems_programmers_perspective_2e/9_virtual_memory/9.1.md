| No. virtual address bits (n) | No. of virtual addresses (N) | Largest possible virtual address  |
|---|---|---|
| 8 | 2^8 = 256 | 2^8 - 1 = 255 |
| 16 | 2^16 = 64K | 2^16 - 1 = 64K - 1 |
| 32 | 2^32 = 4G | 2^32 - 1 = 4G - 1 |
| 48 | 2^48 = 256T | 2^48 - 1 = 256T - 1 |
| 64 | 2^64 = 16384P | 2^64 - 1 = 16384P - 1 |
